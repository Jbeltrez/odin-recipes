Eggplant Cutlet

Description

Easy and adjustable beef stew recipe. Have dinner on the table in under an hour!

    Ingredients

  • 1 ½ Pounds Beef Stew Meat
  • Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • Teaspoon Salt
  • Teaspoon Pepper
  • Teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • Tablespoons Worcestershire Sauce
  • Cloves Garlic, Minced
  • Large Onion, Chopped
  • 16 Ounce Bag Baby Carrots, Cut Into Slices
  • Pound Potatoes, Cubed
  • 2 ½ Cups Beef Broth
  • 1 10 Ounce Can Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons Cornstarch
  • 2 Tablespoons Water

    Steps

  1. Add oil to instant pot. Turn pot onto sautee setting
  2. Add onion and garlic and let aromas set in.
  3. Brown meat for five minutes on sautee setting. After browning add remaning vegetables and stock.
  4. Cover pot with lid and seal pressure valve. Set to high pressure setting for 20 mins.
  5. After the time is up let pressure naturally release. The valve will automatically unseal once all the pressure has released.
  6. Serve hot with a side of white rice.
